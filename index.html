About Me

Greetings! I'm Aldhern, a self taught Flutter Developer. My goal is to become a good Android Developer, and Flutter looked like the best option to start this journey. I'm excited to go on this wild journey, and I'm glad to have you along for the ride!

I hold a Bachelor's in Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Technological University, Kerala. Prior to that, I completed ICSE and ISC examinations with a distinction and a first class grades, respectively. I have always had an interest towards Computer Science in my learning days, which made it easier for me to choose this field for my Bachelor's degree. I did learn valuable lessons during those years which does help me in my day to day life.

Coming to my works, I've currently hosted the completed projects on Google Play Store. The apps I've published/ is currently working on ranges from helpful tools like a money manager, to novelty apps like a random pokemon data generator. I enjoy making apps that come to life with the help of coding (and lots of debugging, lol) - so I'll always be working on something. While working, I tend to prioritize writing clean and comprehensible code, which helps with seamless collaboration and future understanding.

While Flutter development is what I consider my immediate future to be at, it isn't the end goal for me. I value good education, and as such I would be aiming to enhance my professional acumen and elevate my career trajectory through the pursuit of an MBA. If all goes well, I envision complementing my tech expertise with a law degree. This combo would be my way of staying on top of the ever-changing tech industry, helping me navigate legal aspects and make informed decisions in this dynamic landscape.

Acknowledging the strain of extended screen hours, I've intentionally cultivated a range of hobbies to foster a more balanced and fulfilling lifestyle beyond the digital grind. Gardening is one of those hobbies that I picked up along the way. I've managed to grow and sustain a diverse set of plants for my garden, and I'm super proud of accomplishing that. Football is also another one of my favourite activities to spend my free time on, whether it be playing or watching. My latest endeavour when it comes to hobbies is me learning the art of music by mastering playing guitar.

I do enjoy bringing app ideas to life through the magic that is coding. As such, I'm always open to ideas and suggestions regarding this. Feel free to reach out if you have exciting ideas or career opportunities to discuss. Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. I look forward to potential collaborations and exciting opportunities!
