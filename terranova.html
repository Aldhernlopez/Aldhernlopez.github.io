Terranova - Your Guide To Maintaining A Great Garden

This app is meant to offer my takes and experiences growing certain plants during my gardening journey. It's also meant to help bring out the enterpreneur side of mine by offering propagations from my garden at a reasonable price.

Currently, the idea is for the app to work offline, excluding the shop section. The aim is to bring in features like starring/favouriting care guides, various cart functionalities, etc. Stripe will probably be the payment gateway (unless I come across something else).

The app is made with the help of Flutter and will probably be using Firebase as the backend (unless I find a better alternative).
