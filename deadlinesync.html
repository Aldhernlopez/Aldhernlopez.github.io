Chronosync - Stay Ahead of Your Deadlines

This app is designed to help you track all the deadlines in your lives. With it's simple UI, keeping track of deadlines have never been easier.

Users are able to add various types of activities and deadlines and the app will keep track of tem all. By implementing local notification system, users will be getting notified when any deadline is about to be met. Isar is used to provide the backend services, so all data is saved locally. Future updates would bring in features like homescreen widgets and data migration services.
