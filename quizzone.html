Trivia Central - A Quiz Hosting App.

This app idea was based on a few instances during quizzes I used to take part/host in a private Discord Server. There were multiple instances where multiple participants guessed the answer at the same time, and the message hierarchy differed in their own devices. So I thought of this app as a means to fix that age old issue, lol.

The idea is to display a certain number of questions to each of the participants, and then record their individual response time. Since the time will be measured in milliseconds, the chances of two responses happening at the same time will be massively reduced. This custom quiz option will be exclusive to the Discord server, since hosting multiple custom quizzes in case of a large number of userbase could, in theory, create a huge storage bill.

It'll also have a guest login feature, where users will be able to take part in a random quiz in a couple of topics available. They will then get an analysis of their performance, while learning more about certain topics. This way, users will be able to get a taste of how the app works, instead of not being to use it at all.
